About us


Welcome to Hmadie's loungee Located in the heart of the city, our coffee shop and shisha lounge is a sanctuary for relaxation and indulgence
. Our founder, a passionate coffee and shisha lover, envisioned a place where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee meets the soothing scent of premium shisha.
Thus, Hmadie's was born. Step inside to experience a perfect blend of modern elegance and cozy comfort. Our menu features top-quality coffee sourced from the best farms, crafted by skilled baristas.
Our shisha selection offers a variety of flavors to suit every taste, with knowledgeable staff ready to enhance your experience. More than just a place to drink,
we're a community hub hosting events like open mic nights and coffee tastings. Join us at Hmadie's to escape, savor, and enjoy the finer things in life. Welcome to your new favorite spot.

Food

Sirloin offers a rich, beefy flavor with a subtle nutty undertone, balanced by its moderate fat content. It is tender yet firm, providing a juicy bite when cooked properly. The clean and savory aftertaste, along with its adaptability to various seasonings and marinades, makes sirloin a versatile and satisfying choice for steak lovers.

Drinks

A Piña Colada offers a delightful mix of tropical flavors, combining the sweet and tangy taste of pineapple juice with the creamy and smooth texture of coconut cream or milk. The addition of white or light rum provides a gentle warmth and a mild alcoholic kick, creating a harmonious blend that is both refreshing and indulgent. With its fruity aroma and subtle nutty undertones, the Piña Colada is the perfect drink for relaxing and enjoying a taste of the tropics.

Deserts

A Portuguese tart with sticky cinnamon honey delivers a decadent taste experience. The tart features a crisp, flaky pastry crust that encases a rich, creamy custard filling, known for its sweet and slightly eggy flavor. The addition of sticky cinnamon honey drizzled on top infuses the tart with warm, spicy notes of cinnamon and a luscious, sweet stickiness from the honey, perfectly complementing the smooth custard and adding a delightful complexity to each bite.

Shisha

Adalya Love 66 offers a captivating and exotic flavor experience. This shisha blend features a harmonious mix of tropical fruits, with dominant notes of juicy passionfruit and sweet melon, complemented by hints of tangy berries and refreshing mint. The result is a well-balanced, vibrant taste that is both sweet and slightly tart, with a cool, minty finish that adds a refreshing twist to the overall fruity profile.
